Introduction

It is a 4 year programme offered and controlled by the Faculty of Management (FoM), Tribhuwan University, Kirtipur, Kathmandu, Nepal. The objectives of the BBS programme are to develop students into competent managers for any sector of organized activity. The programme is based on the principles that graduates will spend a major portion of their life in a constantly changing environment. The student should have an opportunity to obtain a broad knowledge of the concepts and reality-based skills underlying the operation and management of organizations.
